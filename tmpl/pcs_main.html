The Pie Crust Shell is a Progressive Rock group that kicks ass.

Daniel Gonzalez is a drummer / composer / producer from Mexico City. He began his musical studies in California College of Music and he is currently enrolled in Berklee College Of Music. He has studied with big industry musicians like Quentin Dennard (B.B. King), Ryan Brown (Sex Tapes, Fuxedos), Uros Raskovski (Five Finger Death Punch), Ray Yslas (Christina Aguilera, Colbie Caillat) Walter Miranda (Beastie Boys) Eguie Castrillo (Tito Puente, Jennifer Lopez) and Rod Morgenstein (Jordan Rudess, Dixie Dregs). Daniel has made music for Mexican Television, one of his songs was used as the main theme for a sports show broadcasted in national television. He has produced music for short documentaries and TV notes. Daniel released his debut album on February, 2012 and he is currently working in the post production of his second release "Synesthesia" coming up this last quarter of the year.
